======================================================================
KEY INSIGHT
======================================================================

  Model Fit (HMM vs Markov):
    ΔLL  = +86.1 (HMM better fit)
    ΔAIC = -172.1 (HMM preferred)

  beta_GS Estimation Bias:
    Cox PH (no Z):        +0.0578
    Markov (no Z):        +0.0581
    HMM (with Z):         +0.2222

  → Cox and Markov underestimate GxE interaction
  → HMM corrects for unmeasured confounding (latent Z)
  → HMM shows BEST model fit (highest LL, lowest AIC)
